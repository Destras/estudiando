Simplified Josephus Given n people find the survivor, starting from the first person he kills the person to the left and the next surviving person kills the person to his left, this keeps happening until 1 person survives return that person's number. Examples josephus(1) ➞ 1 josephus(8) ➞ 1 josephus(41) ➞ 19 Notes You can search up the problem if you are too confused about the instructions.
